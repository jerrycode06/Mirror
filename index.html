Who are we?

World is a great mirror and so as you it reflects back to you what you are. They don't show you what's inside, their dark secrets. Mental health is not a destination but a process. It's about how you drive, not where you're going. It's a disorder not a decision. The day you will realize that your baggage was heavy and it was meant to share and one day you will loose all that baggage and you will find happiness in someone or maybe something . Respecting that , we just want to spread awareness that anything can cause emotional imbalance and one should never be afraid to talk about it or to share it with an expert . We believe in helping each other .

Why you should join us?

Life maybe unstable sometimes. You are doing something which you dont want to . You are achieving the goals which are not yours . You are vulnerable. You are lost . You sometimes doesn't even recognize yourself anymore because of the thoughts inside you . This is how life works . You lose yourself but you never forget what you did , what you have been through. This is the beauty of your mental stability but sometimes it needs support , even the bravest soldiers have wounds and that is what makes them the strongest . Embrace those wounds , those thoughts inside you
